Deleting Your Account

Effective Date: January 13, 2026

App: Brain Dump
Developer: aevaksnes

We respect your right to control your data. If you wish to discontinue using Brain Dump, you can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1: Delete via App (Recommended)

This is the fastest way to delete your data. It happens immediately.

  1. Open the Brain Dump app.
  2. Go to the Settings page.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice.

Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by email.

Please send an email from the address associated with your account to:
developing.aevaksnes@gmail.com

Please allow up to 7 days for manual processing of email requests.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

Deleting your account is a permanent action. Once completed:

  • Your login credentials (email/password link) will be removed.
  • All your saved Journal Entries (Dumps) will be permanently deleted from our database.
  • All your custom Prompt Lists will be permanently deleted.
  • This action cannot be undone.
