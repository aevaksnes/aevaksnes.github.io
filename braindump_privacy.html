Privacy Policy for Brain Dump

Effective Date: October 26, 2025

aevaksnes ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Brain Dump mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information for one purpose: to provide and improve our Service to you.

A. Data You Provide Directly

  • User Content: We collect and store the prompts you create, save, and manage within the app. This is the core function of the app. This data is stored in your own secure user-specific collection in our database.

B. Data We Collect Automatically (via Firebase)

When you use our Service, we use Firebase (a Google service) which may collect the following information:

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in using your Google Account, we receive your name, email address, and a unique User ID (UID) from Google. We use this to create your account, secure your data, and identify you as a unique user.
  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how our users interact with the Service. This helps us fix bugs and improve the app. This data is anonymized and may include your device type, operating system, and in-app events (like which screens you visit or buttons you tap).
  • Crash & Performance Data: We use Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix app crashes. If the app crashes, it may send a report including device information, OS version, and the state of the app at the time of the crash.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service (i.e., to save and retrieve your prompts).
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To identify and fix app crashes and bugs.
  • To analyze app usage so we can improve the user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

Our only third-party service provider is **Google (Firebase)**. We share data with them only to operate the Service. For example:

  • Your **authentication data** is managed by Firebase Authentication.
  • Your **prompts** are stored on Google's Firestore database.
  • Your **analytics and crash data** is collected by Firebase.

This data is processed by Google on our behalf. You can review Google's Privacy Policy here.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is critical. We rely on Firebase's robust security measures to protect your information. We have also configured our database (Firestore) with security rules to ensure that **only you can access, read, or write your own data**.

5. Your Data Rights & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual prompts from within the app at any time.

To comply with Google Play policies, we provide two ways to permanently delete your account and all associated data (including all of your saved prompts):

  • 1. From Within the App: You can permanently delete your account by navigating to the "Settings" page inside the app and selecting the "Delete Account" option.
  • 2. Via Our Web Request Form: You can also request account deletion by visiting our account deletion webpage at https://aevaksnes.github.io/braindump_deletion.html or by emailing us directly at aevaksnes@gmail.com.

Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. We will delete all data associated with your account, such as your user profile and all saved prompts.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
