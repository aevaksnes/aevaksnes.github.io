Account Deletion for Shared Kitchen

We are sad to see you go, but we fully support your right to control your data. You can permanently delete your Shared Kitchen account and all associated data in two ways.

Please Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. All of your kitchens, posts, comments, and uploaded images will be permanently erased from our servers.

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Fastest)

The easiest and fastest way to delete your account is directly within the SharedKitchen app:

  1. Open the SharedKitchen app on your device.
  2. Go to the main home page (where you see your kitchens and favorites).
  3. Tap the **Settings** icon (the ⚙️ gear) in the top-right corner.
  4. On the Settings page, scroll to the "Danger Zone" section at the bottom.
  5. Tap on **"Delete Account"** and follow the confirmation prompts.

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Method 2: Manual Deletion via Email

If you no longer have the app installed or cannot access it, you can request manual deletion by sending us an email.

Send an email to:

developing.aevaksnes@gmail.com


Please use the following details:

  • Subject Line: `Account Deletion Request`
  • Email Body: Please state clearly that you wish to delete your account and provide the **email address** you use to log in with Google. This is required so we can find and verify your account.

We will process your request and permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
